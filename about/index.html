About

Who are you?

This site is made by volunteers and it's not officialy part any phone manufacturer, operator or Mozilla Corporation. Builds are hosted by third party servers, we just list them for user convenience.

What does these builds include?

These builds have been created from "vanilla" Firefox OS code. No custom code has been added except from the update URL.

Why my device is not supported?

We try to generate builds for Firefox OS devices where the manufacter does not provide official updates, but in some cases they are locked and it's very complicated. We are working on ways to unlock these devices.

Will I get updates? How?

The builds are ready to provide OTA updates from our custom url, but in this first phase we are not ready yet. You will have to download and install the updates manually.

OTA updates will provide updates using the stable channel. This means 1.3 and then 1.4, 2.0... as soon as they are ready for the devices. Some devices will need more work to support future versions.

How can I help?

The easiest way is to join our testers team downloading and reporting issues.
